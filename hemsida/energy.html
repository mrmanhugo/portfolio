Energy & Emissions BI

Country-level warehouse and Power BI model for production, consumption and emissions with time series measures.

Stack: SQL, Power BI, DAX
Model: Star schema
Focus: Comparisons, trend analysis

Overview

Built a simple warehouse and report to compare countries across energy KPIs and emission trends.

Model & Measures

  • Dimensions for country, energy source and time.
  • Measures for YoY, rolling 12 and intensity per capita.

Outcome

Fast exploration and clear storytelling with consistent definitions and clean visuals.
